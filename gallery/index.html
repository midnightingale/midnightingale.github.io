sunset mindset

last updated september 16, 2024

There’s an obvious poetry in the sunset. She returns with a different dress on each night, flirts with the clouds for a dazzling half hour, then slips away as quickly as she came. By the time I’m drunk on her presence, she’s already gone — how could I love another muse? She could stand me up seven nights in a row, and I’d be back on the eighth.
